Welcome to Makamaka — Barceloneta's beach life meeting point for active people who love real food, fresh drinks and an all-year-round summer vibe. Located in the heart of Barceloneta, Makamaka serves mouthwatering protein burgers, fresh bowls, healthy wraps, power smoothies and crafted cocktails inspired by the vibrant energy of Barcelona's beach culture.

Whether you come after the beach, after a swim, surf session or run, for a quick bite, a long lunch with friends or sunset cocktails on the terrace with sea views, Makamaka combines relaxed beach energy, fresh ingredients, real hospitality and feel-good comfort food in one of Barcelona's most iconic seaside neighborhoods.

An all-day sunshine getaway for locals, travelers, surfers, runners, creatives & beach lovers.
