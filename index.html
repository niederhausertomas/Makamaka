Barceloneta · Est. 2012

Barceloneta's beach life meeting point for active people who love real food, fresh drinks and an all-year-round summer vibe.

A vibrant beach bar and healthy restaurant serving protein burgers, fresh salads, healthy bowls, wraps, power smoothies and crafted cocktails — after the beach, for a quick bite, or sunset cocktails on the terrace with sea views.

An all-day sunshine getaway for locals, travelers, surfers, runners, creatives & beach lovers.

Since 2012

Our Story

Makamaka was born in Barceloneta in 2012, out of a friendship that started years earlier in Amsterdam — between Andrea, a familiar face in the international cocktail scene, and Steef, a seasoned architect and conceptual madman. When Andrea got the chance to open his own place in Barcelona, he brought Steef in, together with a crew of Dutch friends who shared the same love for good food, great people and girls on rollerskates.

“Makamaka.” In Hawaiian, intimate friend.

It's really how it all started — and to this day, Makamaka serves as a 70's-inspired watering hole for locals and travelers alike to share burgers, cocktails and plenty of besos.

The Makamaka Mission

Tasty vibes. Happy burgers. Saucy rollergirls.

Fueled by smiles, friendship & good vibes — we chase the little details, from the products we serve to the family that hosts our guests, because every smile helps make a happier planet.
