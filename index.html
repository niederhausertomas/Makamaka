Welcome to Makamaka — Barceloneta's beach life meeting point for active people who love real food, fresh drinks and an all-year-round summer vibe. Located in the heart of Barceloneta, Makamaka is a vibrant beach bar and healthy restaurant serving mouthwatering protein burgers, fresh salads, healthy bowls, wraps, power smoothies and crafted cocktails inspired by the vibrant energy of Barcelona's beach culture.

Whether you stop by after the beach, a swim, surf session or run, for a quick take away bite, a long lunch with friends or sunset cocktails on the terrace with sea views, Makamaka combines relaxed beach energy, fresh ingredients, real hospitality and feel-good comfort food in one of Barcelona's most iconic seaside neighborhoods.

An all-day sunshine getaway for locals, travelers, surfers, runners, creatives & beach lovers.

Our Story

Makamaka Beach Burger Café was born in Barceloneta in 2012, but the friendship among its creators goes further back. First there was Andrea, a familiar face in the international cocktail scene. Then there was Steef, a seasoned architect and conceptual madman. The two started off as housemates in Amsterdam, where they became fast friends. When Andrea got the chance to open his own place in Barcelona, he didn't forget his promise to involve his friend — the duo teamed up with a crew of Dutch guys who share the same passion for good food, great people and girls on rollerskates.

The love child of it all was dubbed "Makamaka", a name given to Andrea by Steef. In Hawaiian it means intimate friend — which is really how it all started. To this day, Makamaka serves as a 70's-inspired watering hole for locals and travelers alike to share burgers, cocktails and plenty of besos.

The Makamaka Mission

Tasty vibes. Happy burgers. Saucy rollergirls.

Makamaka is fueled by smiles, friendship & good vibes. We try to get people's teeth out through every aspect of what we do, from the products we serve to the family that hosts our guests. We are burger-lovers who believe in the little details, and that every smile helps make a happier planet.
